Help your wallet. Save your money.

Since many people buy clothing that is complete waste, this website acts as a guide as how to spend money responsibly on clothing.

Go to tips

How should I save?

Tip 1: Buy what you need, not want.

Buying clothing that you'll never wear more than once, or buying super fancy tracksuits isn't the best choice. Buy what is affordable and something you can wear more than once.

Tip 2: Buy for now, not for the future.

Chances are, if you buy a suit for a wedding, you'll never wear it. Make sure to question yourself before buying something, "Will I wear this again?".

Tip 3: Buy from thrift stores.

It's really the best way to buy clothing. Cheap, doesn't have an affect on the environment, and reduces waste.

Why Thrift Shops are the Best Option

Benefit 1: Affordable Clothing

Thrift shops offer discounted prices compared to buying new items, allowing you to get great clothing without spending much money.

Benefit 2: Unique Styles

There are a lot of unique items. Maybe you're trying to find something different. "I once went to a thrift store, and I saw some really cool looking glasses."

Benefit 3: Impact Free

Shopping at thrift stores helps reduce waste. Since you're buying secondhand, you are supporting a more safer approach to buying clothes.

Benefit 4: Support Local Communities

Many thrift shops are ran by local charities. You buying from them supports these community organizations and gives back to the people in need.
